Haunted Houses Across the US

There have been many documented hauntings throughout the United States. Claims of seeing shadows walk through the halls late at night, or the uneasy feeling that someone or something is watching. Some hauntings are more famous than others, creating very lucrative tourist traps. People come from all over, to visit these supposedly haunted places.
